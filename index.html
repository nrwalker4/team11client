About us

Smart Gardening Association is a non-profit organization that aims to help people grow their own plants and vegetables. We provide information about plants and gardening tools. We also have a plant finder that can help you find the right plant for your garden.

Plant of the Week

This week's featured plant is the beautiful and easy-to-grow Sunflower! With its yellow, bright leaves and low maintenance requirements, the sunflower is a great addition to any home or office. There's no plant as bright and warm as a sunflower!
